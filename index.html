About-Us

The METMASS, MNIT Jaipur is a society of students, faculty, staff and alumni of the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering. The major objective of METMASS is to promote student - faculty interaction by inter/intra-departmental activities and events and to work together for elevating and propagating awareness in field of metallurgical engineering through a wide spectrum of activities and events. The society aims to further increase the high level interaction existing among the students, the faculty members and researchers working in the field.
